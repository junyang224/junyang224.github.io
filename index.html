About Me

I am a Research Scientist at Epson Canada working on robotics, computer vision, and machine learning. My research focuses on robot perception, 3D vision, neural rendering, SLAM, multi-modal foundation models and robotic manipulation. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Toronto Institute for Aerospace Studies (UTIAS), where I was advised by Prof. Steven L. Waslander. My long-term research goal is to develop intelligent robotic systems that perceive, understand, and interact with complex real-world environments.
